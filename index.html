About Me

I am Harshdip Singh, a healthy and caring individual from India. I came to Canada in 2017 to pursue my higher education because there were better opportunities. I have worked hard to be here and am extremely lucky to get this opportunity to study Computer Science. I have made many projects on computer be it multimedia or small programmes, and I have a great interest in creative writing. I love watching movies and short films and interpret my views on the them, imagining how these forms of entertainment are created. and all these experiences have me today who I am, a guy who loves life and creating original entertainment forms. As a co-op student studying computer science, I hope to gain industry relevant work experience. I am driven and independent, and can work effectively in a any environment, group or individual. I can multitask and effectively prioritize to accomplish objectives with a positive attitude and strong work ethic. I am open-minded to new learning experiences and would like the opportunity to gain industry-specific knowledge. I am greatly interested in editing softwares and computer programming languages, and I have never stopped learning. I was introduced to computer programing when I was 8 years old, and I had learnt HTML by the age of 9, all through online means. Editing softwares greatly interest me, I love to create stuff and I am proficient in world-class standard Adobe products. I have many plans for my future, and I know I am going in the right direction. As of now I want to get a good job while focusing on my grades, and get a student-exchange in my third year. I also want to be a teaching assistant from next year and I am working hard on my grades to be on an honor roll.
